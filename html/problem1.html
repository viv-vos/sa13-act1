Simple Chocolate Cake

Description:

Light, fluffy, airy. Topped with creamy, thick cream cheese frosting. This chocolate cake will bring back nostalgia of birthday cakes from your childhood. It is easy simple and only has a few ingredients to whip up a quick and easy cake! Bring out your creative side in different baking pans and dye your frosting different colors to enjoy this cake for any celebration!!!

Estimated Time: 1 hour and 30 minutes

Recipe:

Ingredients:

Chocolate Cake

  • Boxed chocolate cake
  • 2 sticks of butter
  • ¾ cup milk
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla
  • 3 large eggs
Cream Cheese Frosting
  • 4 oz of Cream Cheese, at room temperature
  • 2 sticks of butter
  • 2 Tbsp of Unsalted Butter, at room temperature
  • ½ tsp of Vanilla Extract
  • 1 to 2 Tbsp of Milk
  • 2 cups of Confectioner Sugar

Steps:

    Cake:

  1. Mix all ingredients together and bake as instructed

    Cream Cheese Frosting:

  1. In a large bowl cream together the butter, cream cheese and vanilla, add the confectioner sugar and continue to whisk until it’s all incorporated.
  2. Add the milk and whisk until everything is combined. Cover with plastic wrap for 1 hour before using.
  3. Use this frosting to top any cupcake, cake or cookie.
